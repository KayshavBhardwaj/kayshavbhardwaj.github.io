Overview

As Co-Lead of the Transcription Project at AI4Musicians Lab, I'm working at the intersection of artificial intelligence and music technology. Our mission is to develop cutting-edge solutions for automated music transcription, making music more accessible and analyzable for musicians and researchers alike.

Key Achievements

• Leading a dynamic team of 10 researchers and developers
• Organizing and developing a competition framework for polyphonic music transcription
• Preparing research papers on advanced transcription methodologies
• Implementing state-of-the-art deep learning models for audio analysis
